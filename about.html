About Team 17670

We are Raider Robotics, the robotics team of Scotch Plains Fanwood High School. Our team competes in FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC), a robotics competition for grades 7-12 run under FIRST, an organization dedicated to student involvement in robotics and STEM.

The team is in the NorthEast Edge League and usually has one competition per month from November to January. In February our league meet takes place - determining if the team moves on to the state-wide competition. While our team travels to other high schools for most competitions, we host the December meet at Scotch Plains Fanwood High School. Check out the dates below to see if you can come and support the team!

about FTC

What is FIRST Tech Challenge?

FIRST Tech Challenge teams (up to 15 team members, grades 7-12) are challenged to design, build, program, and operate robots to compete in a head-to-head challenge in an alliance format.

Guided by adult coaches and mentors, students develop STEM skills and practice engineering principles, while realizing the value of hard work, innovation, and working as a team (FirstInspires.Org).
