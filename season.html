Season focus: Decode

Each competition season, FTC releases a new game where teams must build robots to complete different tasks to score points. This year's game is named Decode. In Decode you must pick up this years game element, Artifacts and launch them into the goal to score. Once in the goal they must pass through the square into the ramp were they will be blocked by the gate. Once the ramp is full Artifacts will go into overflow and will be worth less points. The gate can be opened by the robots allowing the balls to be put back on the field and allowing Artifacts to stay in the ramp and be worth more points. At the end, robots must go to there bases and extend to fit two robots (there alliance partner inside the 18 by 18 base for more points.

Game overview

Decode objectives, step-by-step

Artifact cycles

Collect Artifacts, launch to the goal, and keep the ramp from overflowing to preserve point value.

Gate & ramp

Open the gate to clear the ramp when full, returning Artifacts to the field for more points.

Endgame base

Park and extend inside the 18” base footprint with both alliance robots for endgame bonuses.

Robot goals

What we’re building toward

Hardware priorities

  • Consistent intake to handle multiple Artifacts quickly
  • Turret tuned for goal accuracy and repeatability
  • Drivebase built for cycle speed and precise alignment
  • Fast gate interaction with minimal cycle interruption

Software roadmap

  • Autonomous PedroPaths for reliable early scoring
  • Vision For obilisk readout and acurate goal placement
  • Driver aids: presets, slow-mode, and auto-align helpers
  • Tune subsystems to improve performance
